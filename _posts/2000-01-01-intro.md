Aikido Kokikai of Frederick County, MD has provided classes in the art of <a href="http://www.kokikaiusa.org" target="_blank"> Kokikai Aikido</a>
to the Frederick County community and surroundings since 1997.
Both <a href="https://anc.apm.activecommunities.com/frederickcntyparksandrec/activity/search?onlineSiteId=0&activity_select_param=2&activity_keyword=aikido&viewMode=list" target="_blank">beginner and
intermediate</a> classes are offered on Saturday mornings. We also hold a <a href="https://apm.activecommunities.com/frederickcntyparksandrec/Activity_Search?detailskeyword=aikido+demo&IsAdvanced=True&ddlSortBy=Activity+name&DaysOfWeek=0000000&SearchFor=2&SearchLevelID=2&maxAge=100&NumberOfItemsPerPage=50&IsSearch=true" target="_blank">demo</a> quarterly, but this is still on hold due to COVID-19.

### COVID-19 Precautions

As of February, 2022, we still require masks for practice. We also strongly recommend that participants follow common sense 
cleanliness and hygiene procedures like hand washing, keeping nails trimmed and clean, and wearing clean clothes, for the
safety and well-being of all practitioners.
