Aikido Kokikai of Frederick County, MD has provided classes in the art of <a href="http://www.aikidokokikai.com" target="_blank"> Kokikai Aikido</a>
to the Frederick County community and surroundings since 1997.
 <a href="https://anc.apm.activecommunities.com/frederickcntyparksandrec/activity/search?locale=en-US&activity_select_param=2&activity_keyword=aikido%20for%20beginners&viewMode=list" target="_blank">Beginner</a> classes are offered Saturday mornings year round, and
<a
href="https://anc.apm.activecommunities.com/frederickcntyparksandrec/activity/search?locale=en-US&activity_select_param=2&activity_keyword=intermediate%20aikido&viewMode=list">
intermediate</a> classes are offered on Saturday mornings and Wednesday evenings (these classes are by invitation only once participants have completed the beginner class(es) and shown sufficient profiency in basic falling skills to be able to practice safely). We also hold a <a href="https://anc.apm.activecommunities.com/frederickcntyparksandrec/activity/search?locale=en-US&activity_select_param=2&activity_keyword=aikido%20demo&viewMode=list" target="_blank">demo</a> a week before the start of every beginner session.
